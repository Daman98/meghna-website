Microsoft Edge Extensions Revamp

Microsoft Edge | Launched in 2024

Section 1: Why, what & how

Why we are solving

Users during their web experience on Edge face two types of problems caused by extensions. They often want to uninstall an extension, stop it from accessing a site, or understand what the extension is doing.

Business Objective

Increase the extension usage of the browser by bringing in more users with intent, increase extension conversions and contextual searches within Edge, and reduce friction in the post-installation journey.

Section 1: Experience audit

The experience audit reviewed the existing flow, focusing on tasks and subtasks across the extension lifecycle.

  1. Blocking an extension for a particular domain is difficult.
  2. Users struggle when uninstalling or disabling extensions.
  3. Removing a batch of extensions from the browser is cumbersome.

User Goals

  • Easily access and search for extensions.
  • Disable an extension whenever needed.
  • Understand what an extension is doing.
  • Uninstall with minimal friction.

Section 2: Design direction – MVP & northstar vision

Launched changes

  • Updated extension discovery with top categories and curated lists.
  • Improved search, overview pages, and ratings.
  • Introduced clear design patterns for managing extensions within the toolbar.

Northstar vision

A comprehensive management hub where users can browse, install, and manage extensions with contextual actions and rich information.
